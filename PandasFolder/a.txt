The Standard deviation tells us the typical distance of data points from mean

Low deviation:Data is consistent and bunched near the center
High deviation:Data is erratic and spread far from the center

Eg:Both Student A and B have an average Commute of 30 minutes
-Student A(dev=2 mins):Highly predictable(usually 28-32 min)
-Student B(dev=15 mins):Unpredictable(could be 15 or 45 min)


Rare Zone:Zone where Point out of data range(outliers)

To check rare zone point, we use Z score:

Measures how many Standard deviation a point is away"
        Z=(x-mean)/SD
